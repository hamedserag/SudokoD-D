⬢ Events

There are 2 types of Events Positive and Negative randomly activated while you are walking around the dungeon and solving the puzzle

POSITIVE events that help you clear the puzzle faster (heals you gives you more stamina solves boxes etc..)

NEGATIVE events that prevent you from clearing the puzzle (destroying some finished pieces damaging you etc..)
